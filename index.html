Zeus is a fine fast casual Greek restaurant serving up humble street food in a fun, relaxed casual environment that is good for the heart and soul. We import all our our hormone antibiotic free meats from our homeland of Greece and combine it with the freshest produce sourced from our local farms. Live. Eat Greek All our salads are made fresh to order with the finest ingredients and the number one Greek Olive Oil. Live. Eat Greek.
